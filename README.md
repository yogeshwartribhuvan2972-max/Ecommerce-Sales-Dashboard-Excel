# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Dashboard – Excel

An interactive Excel dashboard developed to analyze e-commerce sales performance, customer behavior, payment methods, product categories, and delivery trends.

This project demonstrates the practical use of Microsoft Excel for data cleaning, analysis, visualization, and business insight generation.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The objective of this project is to analyze e-commerce sales data and transform raw data into meaningful business insights through an interactive Excel dashboard.

The dashboard helps users understand:

- Overall sales performance
- Total number of orders
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Average customer rating
- Monthly sales trends
- Payment method distribution
- Product category performance
- City-wise sales performance
- Customer age-group analysis
- Delivery time and customer rating trends

## 📈 Dashboard Analysis

### 1. Monthly Sales Trend

Analyzes monthly sales performance to identify fluctuations and high-performing months.

### 2. Payment Method Analysis

Shows customer preferences across different payment methods:

- Credit Card
- Debit Card
- Net Banking
- UPI

### 3. Category-Wise Sales

Compares sales performance across product categories such as:

- Beauty
- Books
- Clothing
- Electronics
- Home Appliances
- Sports

### 4. City-Wise Sales Analysis

Analyzes sales performance across different cities to identify strong-performing business locations.

### 5. Customer Age-Group Analysis

Compares product sales across different customer age groups, including:

- Teenagers
- Adults
- Seniors

### 6. Delivery Time vs Customer Rating

Analyzes the relationship between delivery time and average customer ratings.

---

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

- Beauty and Books are the top-performing product categories by sales share.

- Bangalore and Delhi show the highest sales performance among the analyzed cities.

- Digital payment methods such as UPI and Credit Cards account for a significant share of customer transactions.

- October recorded the highest monthly sales, while November showed the lowest sales performance.

- Adult customers contribute significantly more sales compared to senior and teenage customer groups.

- The dashboard enables interactive filtering by Payment Method, City, and Product Category.
